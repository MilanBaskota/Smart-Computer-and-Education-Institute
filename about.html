About us

Smart Computer & Education Institute is a leading training center dedicated to empowering students and professionals with practical, industry-relevant skills. Our mission is to bridge the gap between education and employment by providing high-quality computer and professional training programs that prepare learners for real-world careers.


Located at Aryan Plaza, 2nd Floor, Mechinagar-13, Charali, Jhapa, we offer a wide range of job-focused courses designed to enhance technical knowledge, improve workplace efficiency, and increase employment opportunities.

Our Missions

Our mission is to empower individuals with practical knowledge, digital skills, and professional competencies that enable them to succeed in an increasingly technology-driven world. We are committed to providing quality education that goes beyond traditional classroom learning by focusing on hands-on training, real-world applications, and industry-relevant skills.

Our mission is not only to teach skills but also to inspire personal growth, professional excellence, and entrepreneurial thinking. We believe that every individual deserves access to affordable, practical, and career-oriented education that opens doors to new opportunities and a brighter future.
